# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the IDeeps community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the team and the product

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the leadership team at info@ideeps.ai. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
